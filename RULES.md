# Roll for Shoes Rules

**Roll for Shoes** is a tabletop RPG "micro system" created by Ben Wray.

## Core Rules

1.  **Say what you do and roll a number of D6s**, determined by the level of relevant skill you have.
2.  **If the sum of your roll is higher than an opposing roll**, the thing you wanted to happen, happens.
3.  **At start, you have only one skill:** `Do Anything 1`.
4.  **If you roll all 6s**, you get a new skill specific to the action, one level higher than the one you used.
5.  **For every roll you fail**, you get **1 XP**.
6.  **XP can be used to change a die into a 6** for evolution purposes only.

## Evolution & XP

*   **Gaining Skills:** You only gain a new skill when you roll all 6s on a check. The new skill must be a specific subset of the skill you just used (e.g., using "Do Anything 1" to kick a door -> roll a 6 -> gain "Kick Doors 2").
*   **Using XP:** You cannot use XP to modify the *result* of a check (success/failure). You can ONLY use it to change a die face to a 6 *after* the roll to trigger the "All 6s" evolution rule.
